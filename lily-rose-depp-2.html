    Actress and model Lily-Rose Melody Depp are of French and American descent. She comes from a wealthy family and is widely renowned for her acting abilities. She made her acting debut in Tusk in 2014 and went on to star in the historical plays The Dancer, in which she played Isadora Duncan, and The King, in which she played King George VI.

    For her roles in The Dancer and A Faithful Man (2018), Depp has been nominated for two César Awards for Most Promising Actress. At the 4th Los Angeles Film Awards, she received Best Actress for her work in My Last Lullaby.

    She has served as a Chanel brand ambassador since 2015. In addition to this, she has millions of Instagram followers. Many on social media have become fans of her because of her friendly personality. Online, she communicates with a sizable social network and fan base.

    Where was Lily-Rose Depp born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

    West of Paris in the French city of Neuilly-sur-Seine, she was born on May 27, 1999. Her age is 23. She is the child of French singer, actress, and model Vanessa Paradis and American actor, producer, and musician Johnny Depp.

    John “Jack” Christopher Depp III is Depp’s younger brother. Her aunt is the French performer Alysson Paradis. Depp is also a direct descendant of Elizabeth Key Grinstead, a colonial freedom fighter. After spending 14 years together, Depp’s parents separated in 2012, and Depp now lives in both Los Angeles and Paris. She has a strong education.
