    Khloe And Lamar Call Off Divorce To Give Marriage Another Shot

    After a medical emergency last week kept the relationship of Lamar Odom and Khloe Kardashian under a huge microscope, it has been reported by TMZ that the on-again/off-again couple have decided to call off their divorce, which was headed for finalization, and will give another shot at their marriage.

    07 Aug 2024, 00:00

    After a medical emergency last week kept the relationship of Lamar Odom and Khloe Kardashian under a huge microscope, it has been reported by TMZ that the on-again/off-again couple have decided to call off their divorce, which was headed for finalization, and will give another shot at their marriage.

    “Khloe’s lawyer Laura Wasser went before a judge Wednesday morning, asking a judge to withdraw the papers they both signed to end their marriage,” the site reports. “The file was sitting in a pile waiting to be processed and signed by a judge. The judge granted Wasser’s request and the file is now dead.”

    The basketball player and the reality starlet tied the knot in September 2009 after a month of dating, and Kardashian filed for divorce in 2013 after months of speculation from the press. The cause for the split was cited as “irreconcilable differences,” with numerous outlets claiming Odom was having difficulty keeping his drug and alcohol problems under control and that he was cheating on Khloe.

    The severity of his problems were brought to the forefront on Tuesday, Oct. 13 after Odom, 35, was found unconscious at a Las Vegas, Nev. brothel after consuming an unknown amount of different drugs and alcohol. He was placed on life support after slipping into a coma until he regained consciousness a few days later. Kardashian flew to the hospital to be with him and reportedly never left his bedside. He was discharged from the hospital yesterday (Oct. 20), and will have to undergo months of physical therapy in order to help him along his long road to recovery.

    After separating, Khloe was linked to rapper French Montana and most recently the shooting guard for the Houston Rockets, James Harden. Just yesterday (Oct. 20), it was reported that Kardashian, 31, placed her relationship with Harden on the back burner so that she could take care of Odom.

    This proves that the love the two have for each other is extremely strong despite their issues in the past. Hopefully Odom makes a strong recovery and Khloe’s support helps him through it.
