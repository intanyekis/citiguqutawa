    Bianca Belair Career- Wrestling

    Bianca Belair Career: - According to Hurd, Blair uploaded her information into the WWE prospects database "more on a whim than with an actual plan" shortly after her CrossFit career ended prematurely. Less than two weeks later, she received a social media message from 20-year WWE veteran Mark Henry, who saw Blair's profile on the CrossFit circuit and said he could get her a trial, but she had to do the work. Blair signed a contract with WWE on April 12, 2016, after two formal tryouts, and was assigned to the WWE Performance Centre. Blair made her debut on June 25, 2016, during an in-ring segment at an NXT home show. On September 29, 2016, she made her NXT in-ring debut in an NXT live event in a losing effort against Aliyah.

    Blair made her television debut on May 3, 2017 in a battle royal to decide the No. 1 challenger for the NXT Women's Championship, where she was eliminated by Billie Kay and Peyton Royce. Belair beat Aliyah on June 23 to qualify for the Mae Young Classic. Belair beat Sage Beckett in the opening round of the competition on July 13. The next day, Belair was knocked out of the event in the second round by Kairi Sane. On September 20, 2017, Belair made her singles NXT television debut, beating Lacey Evans.
