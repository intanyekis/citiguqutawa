    Twiggy Ramirez Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

    Jeordie Osbourne White (born June 20, 1971), better known by his stage name Twiggy Ramirez, (derived from Twiggy, a fashion icon, and Richard Ramirez, a serial killer) is an American musician, best known as both a bassist and guitarist, particularly within the band Marilyn Manson. Previously, he was the bassist for A Perfect Circle and a touring member of Nine Inch Nails, and is currently the vocalist for Goon Moon. He left Marilyn Manson in 2002, but rejoined the band in 2008. He has been a principal songwriter for the band and has also contributed to some of the Desert Sessions recordings. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
