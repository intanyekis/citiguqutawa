    Net Worth & Income

    Not a new name to golf fanatics, Rickie Fowler has earned an enormous fortune from his super-successful career. As of December 2023, the young golfer Rickie Fowler has a net worth of a whopping $40 million.

    Unlike most mainstream sports, golfers do not earn a fixed and consistent salary. Their income entirely depends on the prize money, and believe it or not, they make tens of millions from a single tour prize money.

    Also, contributing the lion’s share to a golfer’s fortune is brand endorsement deals. Take, for instance, Tiger Woods, whose career earning sits at around $120 million, but with his brand deals and business ventures, he has clinched a billionaire status.

    With total career earnings of $43.26 million, Fowler ranks 21st on professional golfers’ all-time money leaders list.

    If we were to calculate his unofficial payouts and tour championship bonus, his career earnings sit at around $49.47 million as of 2023.

    Brand Endorsements

    Besides earning millions from his Tour prize money, the young golfer Rickie Fowler also makes well from brand deals with several top-tier brands and companies.

    He has promotion deals with brands such as Rolex, TaylorMade, Titleist, and Puma.

    Fowler appeared in a commercial for the Crowne Plaza Hotels campaign titled “It’s Good to be Rickie” in 2012.

    The following year he featured in an ESPN Sportscenter show.

    Rickie was named the ambassador for the PGA Junior League Golf in 2015.

    In 2017, Rickie earned $9.1 million from brand deals, slightly less than the year before when he made $10 million.

    Real Estate

    Besides earning from golf and brand endorsements, Rickie Fowler is also making his name in the real estate game as he has traded a few pieces of properties in the past.

    He listed his Florida house for a cool $2.85 million in 2016, which he had purchased back in 2010 for $1.6 million.

    Soon after listing his former house, Rickie purchased a massive $14 million mansion in Jupiter, Florida, the same year.

     Early Life & Education

    Rickie Fowler was born Rick Yutaka Fowler on December 13, 1988, in Murrieta, California, United States.

    His middle name comes from the maternal side of his family. His maternal grandfather is Japanese, and his maternal grandmother is Navajo Native American.

    For his schooling Rickie attended Murrieta Valley High School. The almost entirely self-taught golfer began his journey playing on a driving range.

    After an impressive high school career, Fowler went to Oklahoma State University in Stillwater and posted his first college victory on October 1, 2007, at the Fighting Illini Invitational hosted by the University of Illinois.

    Wife & Children

    Rickie Fowler has been married to professional American pole-vaulter Allison Stokke since 2019.

    The couple began dating in 2017, and Fowler popped the question the following year. They welcomed their first baby together in 2021, whom they named Maya.

    Talking a bit about Stokke, she grew up in Newport Beach, California, attended Newport Harbor High School, and started pole vaulting in her first year.

    She had an amazing high school career, and during her senior year, she was ranked second in the nation and California for pole vaulting.

    To top it off, she broke American records as a pole vaulter.

    After high school graduation, Stokke received an athletic scholarship to study sociology at the University of California, Berkeley.

    She currently works as a fitness model, and according to Celebrity Net Worth, the American pole vaulter Allison Stokke has a net worth of a whopping $5 million as of December 2023.

    Her fortune can be attributed to her pole vaulting career, even though she began to focus more on modeling later.

    She has appeared in campaigns for Uniqlo, Athleta, and Nike. Similarly, she has a partnership with GoPro, using the camera equipment to make pole vaulting YouTube videos.
