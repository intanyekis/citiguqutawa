    After the tragic passing of Richard Harris, Michael Gambon made his debut as Albus Dumbledore in the third Harry Potter movie. Tragically for Gambon, his family, and his fans, he suffered from health issues for a long time and then passed away in 2023.

    In public, Gambon often came across as a very serious actor. While Gambon clearly took his craft seriously, it turns out he could be a prankster behind the scenes. No matter how surprising Gambon's mischievous nature might be for some fans, that pales in comparison to how shocking his love life was.

    The Media Has Obsessed Over The Love Lives Of The Harry Potter Cast

    When the world learned that the Harry Potter books were going to be adapted for the big screens, fans couldn't wait to see who would be cast. Most notably, millions of fans were extremely curious to learn who would portray Harry, Hermione, and Ron.

    By the time the Harry Potter movies came to an end, one of Potter's young stars was getting a lot more attention. No matter how much Emma Watson tried to get the tabloids to leave her alone, the media was obsessed with her for many years.

    Most notably, the media seemed to salivate any time that new information about Watson's love life surfaced. For that reason, it sometimes seemed like the media wanted to claim that Watson was dating any young man she was seen with.

    Who Are Michael Gambon's Wives And Kids?

    In the modern world of social media, tabloids, and gossip websites, it often seems like fans know everything about their favorite star's love life. In many cases, that is the case because many celebrities now seem to share every aspect of their love life on social media.

    In years past, however, there were a lot of celebrities who did everything they could to maintain their privacy. An example of that elder group before his passing, Michael Gambon avoided talking about his personal life as much as he could.

    According to The Mirror, Gambon once took extreme measures to keep his love life out of the spotlight. During an interview, Gambon was asked about his longtime wife, and he responded to the question by asking, "What wife?"

    If that response had anyone questioning if Gambon actually was married, his passing ended the mystery. Once Gambon took his final breath, a statement was released on behalf of his wife and their son Fergus Gambon mourning the famed actor.
