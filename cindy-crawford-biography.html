    Who is Cindy Crawford?

    Cindy Crawford is an American supermodel as well as an actress. She appeared in almost 500 covers which also include popular covers such as Vogue, Elle, Cosmopolitan, Harper’s Bazaar, and much more.

    Furthermore, she has also walked for big names such as Chanel, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, Christian Dior, Calvin Klein, Ralph Lauren, and much more.

    Besides this, she has also starred in several films and television series, including Sunrise, According to Jim, The Hospital In The Sky, Fair Game, The Simian Line, and few more.

    Who are the parents of Cindy Crawford?

    Cindy Crawford was born on February 20, 1966, in DeKalb, Illinois, USA. She is the daughter of Jennifer Sue Crawford-Moluf and John Daniel Crawford.

    She has three siblings, two sisters Chris and Danielle and a brother Jeffery. Her brother died of childhood leukemia at age 3.

    Talking about her nationality, she is American and her ethnicity is mixed (English, Scottish, German, Irish, and Welsh). As a child, she developed her interest in modeling and began to appear for various brands from a very early age.

    Moving towards her education, Cindy completed her graduation from DeKalb High School in 1984. Later, she also earned an academic scholarship to study chemical engineering at Northwestern University. Although, she later dropped out just after a year to pursue her modeling career.

    Cindy Crawford: Career, Net Worth, Awards

    Cindy Crawford began her modeling career at a young age of 17 when she took part in the Elite Model Management‘s Look of the Year contest. In the competition, she also came as a runner-up. Since then, Elite Model Management began to represent her.

    Being an American supermodel, she appeared in almost 500 covers which also include popular covers such as Vogue, Elle, Cosmopolitan, Harper’s Bazaar, and much more.

    Furthermore, she has also walked for big names such as Chanel, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, Christian Dior, Calvin Klein, Ralph Lauren, and much more. Back in 1987, she appeared during the opening credits of the Michael J.

    Fox film The Secret of My Success. A couple of years later in 1990, Cindy was featured along with top models Christy Turlington, Linda Evangelista, Tatjana Patitz, and Naomi Campbell on the cover of British Vogue magazine.

    Besides modeling, this veteran model also appeared in Jon Bon Jovi music video “Please Come Home For Christmas” as well as Bad Blood by Taylor Swift.

    In addition, she is also a fitness expert. Furthermore, some of her fitness videos are Cindy Crawford: Shape Your Body Workout, and Cindy Crawford: The Next Challenge Workout.

    Apart from her modeling, she is also an actress and also starred in a couple of movies as well as in television shows. Some of her popular roles in movies and TV shows include Sunrise, According to Jim, The Hospital In The Sky, Fair Game, The Simian Line, and a few more.

    Additionally, Cindy is an activist who regularly takes part and donates to the leukemia patient. She is on the honorary committee of the California Wildlife Center.

    Being a legendary model and actress, Cindy pockets a massive amount of money from her profession. Currently, she has a whopping amount of net worth $100 million.

    As of now, Cindy Crawford has not won any awards. Although, she has gotten a couple of nominations for the Razzie Award and Stinker Award.

    In addition, her other body measurements include 36 inches bra size, 26 inches waist, and 35 inches hip size.
