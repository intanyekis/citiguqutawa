    About Thrill Builders

    Thrill Builders is a renowned company based in Madison, Wisconsin. They specialize in creating exceptional and captivating custom escape room experiences, haunted attractions, and other thrilling activities. As a B2B service provider, Thrill Builders designs and constructs these attractions for other companies, offering a comprehensive range of services from concept design to installation and training.

    What sets Thrill Builders apart is their meticulous attention to detail. They not only focus on the physical build-outs of these attractions but also provide customized soundtracks and lighting to create an immersive atmosphere. With a portfolio that includes collaborations with major theme parks and events worldwide, such as Walt Disney, Universal, National Geographic, and Six Flags, Thrill Builders has established themselves as industry leaders in concept design and installation.

    Thrill Builders takes pride in being a turnkey solution provider, ensuring their clients receive a complete package for their immersive attractions. They go beyond constructing the physical aspects and offer comprehensive training programs to equip their clients’ staff with the knowledge and skills needed to operate these thrilling facilities successfully.

    With their extensive experience and expertise, Thrill Builders has served nearly 8,000 clients globally, helping businesses in the entertainment industry create unique and unforgettable experiences for their guests.

    Thrill Builders on Shark Tank

    Thrill Builders, the innovative thrill-seeking entertainment company, made a memorable appearance on Season 15, Episode 5 of Shark Tank. Founder Guy Kitchell showcased the company’s range of services, including thrilling escape rooms, haunted attractions, laser tag arenas, mini golf courses, and theming services.

    Kitchell took the opportunity to present his ambitious plan to expand the business with the creation of a large theme park facility called Thrill Factory. Seeking a $250,000 investment in exchange for 20% equity in Thrill Builders, he pitched the concept with enthusiasm and confidence.

    After negotiation, Kitchell successfully secured a deal with three prominent Sharks – Lori Greiner, Kevin O’Leary, and Jason Blum. They agreed to invest $300,000 in exchange for 30% equity in the company, valuing Thrill Builders at an estimated $1.25 million.

    The Thrill Builders’ appearance on Shark Tank not only yielded a significant financial boost but also provided the company with a valuable marketing and exposure opportunity. This exposure has catapulted Thrill Builders into the spotlight, generating increased interest and inquiries from potential customers eager to experience their unique offerings.
