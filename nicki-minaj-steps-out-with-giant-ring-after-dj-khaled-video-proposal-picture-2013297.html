    Nicki Minaj's DJ Khaled Proposal: Rapper Steps Out With Giant Ring

    14 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Is a picture really worth a thousand words? Nicki Minaj has yet to publicly address DJ Khaled's memorable MTV video proposal, but the "Super Bass" singer raised eyebrows when she stepped out to dinner in Beverly Hills on Saturday, July 27, with a new accessory around her ring finger.

    The 30-year-old rapper wore an attention-grabbing ensemble for her evening outing, including a bright neon mesh cap, pink shirt and pastel yellow high-waisted jeans. Minaj brushed her blonde hair aside with her left hand — which boasted an impressive sparkler on her ring finger — multiple times.

    On Thursday, July 25, DJ Khaled made headlines after he recorded a romantic message to Minaj, in which he asked the outspoken rapper for her hand in marriage.

    "I feel like you need a man like me in your life … we got the same symptoms, we're both suffering from success," he said in the video. "[If] you gotta take your time and think about it, I understand, but I know I have to be here today to let you know how serious I am and how serious this is to me."

    Khaled finished off his speech with a heartfelt, "Nicki Minaj, will you marry me?" and a reported $500,000 10-carat ring in a box.

    Though fans were initially confused about whether or not the proposal was genuine, Khaled exclusively assured Us that he is, indeed, serious. The pair have never been photographed together, and there have not been any previous reports of them dating.

    "I want to marry her!" he told Us. "She can take her time and think about it. At least she knows how I feel now."
