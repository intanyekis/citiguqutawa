    Damon Wimbley: A Rapper and Actor with a Net Worth of $400 Thousand

    Damon Wimbley, born in November 1966, is a rapper and actor from the United States. He is best known for being a member of the hip hop trio The Fat Boys, where he was known as “Kool Rock-Ski”. The group was formed in 1982 and broke up in 1991 before reuniting in 2008.

    Their self-titled debut studio album was released in 1984 and was certified Gold. The album reached #6 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart and #48 on the Billboard 200 chart. The Fat Boys had success with their albums The Fat Boys Are Back in 1985 and Big & Beautiful in 1986. Their album Crushin’ in 1987 reached #4 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart and #8 on the Billboard 200 chart. The group also released the albums Coming Back Hard Again in 1988, On and On in 1989, and Mack Daddy in 1991.

    In addition to music, Wimbley also appeared in the movies Knights of the City, Disorderlies, and My Crackhead Uncle. As of now, Damon Wimbley has a net worth of $400 thousand.
