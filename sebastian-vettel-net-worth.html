    Originating from West Germany, Sebastian Vettel is a German Formula One race car driver with an estimated net worth of $55 million in 2015.

    Currently playing for the Red Bull-Renault, Sebastian Vettel is the present World Champion. He won the World Championship thrice in a row from 2010 through 2012. Debuting on Red Bull Renault in 2009, Vettel became the youngest runner up in the World Drivers' championship. In 2010, he won the World Drivers' Championship and on the same year, he helped the team in winning its first World Constructors' Championship. On succeeding year, he won the championship again, making him the youngest consecutive and youngest double champion. With a total of 281 points, Sebastian Vettel won the 2012 F1 World Championship, making him the youngest triple champion in the history of Formula One racing.

    His first race took place in 2007 at the United States Grand Prix and his first win occurred at the 2008 Italian Grand Prix.
