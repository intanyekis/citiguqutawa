    What Is Youtube Ambient Mode?

    The Ambient Mode on YouTube turns the app's background colour in real-time to match the video you are watching using an elusive effect. This is more similar to the effect you can find in gadgets with innovative light solutions that change their background colour relative to what is on the screen.YouTube record states they "wanted to refurbish the effect, so viewers were haggard right into the content, and the video takes an even more focus on our page." The Ambient Mode only works when the dark mode option is enabled on the YouTube app.

    The ambient mode uses a lighting effect to make watching videos in the Dark theme more mesmerising by casting gentle colours from the video into your screen's background.
