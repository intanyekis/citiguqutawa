    Ed began writing songs at a young age and independently released his music before rising to global stardom.

    His musical style spans various genres, and he has cited influences ranging from the Beatles to Eminem.

    In addition to his musical achievements, he received an MBE (Member of the Order of the British Empire) in 2017 for his contributions to music.

    Ed’s career journey showcases his evolution from a street busker to a chart-topping sensation with multiple accolades and a dedicated fan base worldwide.

    Ed Sheeran siblings

    Ed has one sibling, an older brother named Matthew Sheeran, a renowned composer, orchestrator and arranger.
